## Global layout (all pages)

- Layout
    - Max content width around 1200–1360px, centered with generous padding.
    - Clear sections with plenty of white space and a consistent vertical rhythm.
- Header
    - Sticky header with white or very light background and subtle shadow on scroll.
    - Left: Spruce logo.
    - Right: horizontal navigation (Home, Portfolio, Services, About, Contact) + a distinct primary button “Start your project” linking to Contact.
- Footer
    - Three columns on desktop, stacked on mobile.
    - Column 1: Logo + one-sentence description: “Custom residential homebuilder in Richmond \& Virginia’s Northern Neck.”
    - Column 2: Quick links (Home, Portfolio, Services, About, Contact, Boscobel Trace).
    - Column 3: Contact info (address, email) and Instagram link.
- Style
    - Typography:
        - Headings: a refined serif or strong geometric sans.
        - Body: clean sans serif, comfortable line height.
    - Colors:
        - Background: off-white or very light neutral.
        - Accent: Spruce green and warm neutral tones to complement existing logo and photography.
        - Buttons: high-contrast solid color with white text.
